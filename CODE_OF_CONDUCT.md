# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Accepting feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.
